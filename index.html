About Me

Hey, I'm Massee! I am currently finishing my fourth year at the University of California, Irvine. I made the transition from biological sciences to applied physics during the end of my third year, and shortly thereafter, added my mathematics major. I currently have the privilege of beginning my Research Assistantship at Dr. Howard Lee's Nano-Optics Research Laboratory. One of the research group's focus is on the fabrication of epsilon-near-zero materials for revolutionary nano-optical applications to optical communcations and medical imaging. I hope to attend graduate school to obtain my PhD in Applied Physics with research in Nanophotonics.
